üíº About This Course

Accounting for Managers introduces you to the language of business through financial accounting fundamentals. You'll learn to read and analyze financial statements, understand key financial metrics, and make informed managerial decisions based on accounting information.

This course covers assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, and expenses, with practical exercises based on real-world transactions. The final exam is open-book and conducted online, allowing you to demonstrate your comprehensive understanding of the material.
