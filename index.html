About the Course

Welcome to Accounting for Managers (Course 13002)! This course, led by Dr. Shay Tsaban, provides a comprehensive introduction to financial accounting and reporting. It is designed to equip managers with the tools to analyze financial data, understand key financial statements, and make informed economic decisions.

The coursebook contains all necessary information for the semester, structured as lesson summaries. For formal expansions, refer to the official course website and study guide.
